About HellaClean

Sheraz Khan

Hi, I'm Sheraz Khan aka "Hellaz" the founder of HellaClean. I've always been a sneakerhead and valued taking the time to try to make sure my sneakers were presentable and long-lasting. I always wanted to get the most life out of a single pair of sneakers so I began learning and researching more methods and techniques into how and what is most effective and efficient. I bumped into a lot of hurdles and false information along the way. I was finding techniques that had my sneakers looking fresh but the chemicals weer damaging the material or on the contrary; I would find techniques that would maintain the quality of the material but not give a deep enough clean. After years - decades - of practice I opitmised my techniques and also met a lot of people who shared the same passion for fresh kicks. This was when I realised that sneaker restoring is a service which has a demand. Initially, I only wanted to get into reselling brand new sneakers although i felt something holding me back from taking the plunge. I no longer want to explore reselling as I've noticed it doesn't align with my values towards my resposibilty to cut back landfill. Restoring sneakers aligns well with my morals and values.
